Email options provide validated email input fields for collecting customer email addresses.

## Use Cases

- Gift recipient email
- Notification email
- Digital delivery address
- Contact information


## Configuration

```
Label: "Gift Recipient Email"
Validation: Automatic
Required: Yes
```
