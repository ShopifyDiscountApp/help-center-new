Number options provide numeric input with validation, perfect for quantities and measurements.

## Use Cases

- Custom quantities
- Measurements (height, width)
- Age or year
- Numeric codes


## Configuration

```
Min Value: 1
Max Value: 100
Step: 1 (integers)
Default: 1
```
