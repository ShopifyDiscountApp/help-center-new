Optionify offers various plans to suit different business needs, from small stores to large enterprises.

## Available Features

All plans include:
- 11 option types
- 4 assignment methods
- Unlimited options
- Mobile responsive
- Order integration

## Plan Tiers

### Starter Plan
Perfect for small stores just getting started with custom options.

### Professional Plan
For growing businesses with advanced needs like conditional logic.

### Enterprise Plan
For large stores requiring priority support and custom integrations.


## Choosing the Right Plan

Consider:
- Number of products
- Complexity of options needed
- Support requirements
- Budget

## Upgrading

You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from the app dashboard.
