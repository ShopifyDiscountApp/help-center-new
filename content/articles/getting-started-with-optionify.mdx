Welcome to Optionify! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with adding custom product options to your Shopify store.

## What is Optionify?

Optionify is a powerful Shopify app that allows you to add custom options to your products beyond Shopify's standard 3 variant limit. With 11 different option types and 4 flexible assignment methods, you can create unlimited customization possibilities for your customers.

\![Optionify dashboard overview](/images/getting-started/dashboard-overview.png)
*The Optionify dashboard showing available option types and recent configurations*

## Why Use Optionify?

### Overcome Shopify's Variant Limitations

Shopify limits products to 3 options with 100 variants total. Optionify breaks through these limitations by allowing you to:

- Add unlimited custom input fields
- Collect customer personalization data
- Offer product customizations without creating variants
- Gather special instructions and preferences

### Powerful Features

- **11 Option Types**: Text, textarea, radio, switch, checkbox, dropdown, image upload, date picker, email, number, and color
- **4 Assignment Methods**: Apply options to all products, specific tags, collections, or individual products
- **Conditional Logic**: Show or hide options based on customer selections
- **Pricing Integration**: Add extra costs to specific options
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works perfectly on all devices


## Installation

### Step 1: Install from Shopify App Store

1. Go to the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com)
2. Search for "Optionify"
3. Click **Add app**
4. Review the permissions and click **Install app**

\![Installing Optionify from Shopify App Store](/images/getting-started/install-app.png)
*Click "Add app" to begin the installation process*

### Step 2: Grant Permissions

Optionify requires the following permissions to function properly:

- **Read and modify products**: To add custom options to your products
- **Read orders**: To include custom option data in orders
- **Read themes**: To integrate with your store's theme


### Step 3: Complete Initial Setup

After installation, you'll be guided through a quick setup wizard:

1. **Choose Your Plan**: Select the plan that fits your needs
2. **Theme Integration**: Optionify will automatically integrate with most themes
3. **Test Product**: Create a test option to see how it works

\![Setup wizard completion screen](/images/getting-started/setup-wizard.png)
*The setup wizard helps you get started in under 2 minutes*

## Best Practices


### Naming Conventions

Use clear, descriptive names for your options:
- ✅ "Gift Message (optional)"
- ✅ "Engraving Text (max 20 characters)"
- ❌ "Text field 1"
- ❌ "Option A"

### Test Before Launch

Always test new options on your store before making them live:

1. Add the option to a test product
2. Place a test order
3. Verify the option data appears correctly in your order
4. Check mobile responsiveness

### Organization

As you create more options:
- Use consistent naming
- Document what each option does
- Group related options together
- Archive unused options instead of deleting
